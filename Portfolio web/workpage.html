Proverbs in Nikolai

Nikolai, a typeface created by a designer called Franziska Weitgruber. She reinterprets Nebiolo’s Jenson model from the Renaissance Turiner foundry. Franziska was attracted to its wildness and digitized the bold weight of Nebiolo’s Jenson. I chose Proverbs 16 from the Bible to make a Nikolai type specimen. Nikolai suited perfectly in terms of illustrating the legacy of the bible by a unique bold typeface. Using peacock blue as my main color to convey the elegant aesthetic.
